Stop and Frisk Program 2012(Python)

SQF 2012 (Stop and Frisk Program 2012) is program introduced by New York to control the crime rate in different cities. Police officers randomly stopped people on the streets, residential areas, based on the cloths wearing, furtive movements, suspicious bulge, actions of involving in different violent crime inquired searched for different suspicious objects people are carrying such as gun, knife etc. All the information about the person being stopped as race, age, height, city, if any physical force is used to stop people is record in the file for further analysis. Approximately 532,911 people were stopped in 2012.out of which 423168 males, 50000 women were stopped. Average age of people being stopped is 174 feet. More of the black race is targeted by the police officers. 51% of stopping is based on furtive movement. Black and White Hispanic were more likely to be frisked than white and, among those frisked, were less likely to be found with a weapon.
